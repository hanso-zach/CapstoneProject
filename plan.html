Overview

This page offers a quick introduction to the key components of an effective Security and Disaster Recovery Plan. Below are summaries of critical areas that help build a resilient and secure organization.

Risk Assessment

Risk assessment involves identifying potential threats and vulnerabilities, including cyber and physical risks. It helps determine the impact of these threats and supports informed decision-making using models like those from the FAIR Institute. Learn more on the Risk Assessment page.

Security Policies

Effective policies are the foundation of secure operations. Topics include access control mechanisms like MFA and RBAC, encryption practices, and incident response procedures. Full details are available on the Policies page.

Disaster Recovery

The Disaster Recovery Plan outlines strategies to ensure business continuity during crises. It includes recovery objectives, business planning, training, and regular testing. For a full breakdown, visit the Disaster Recovery page.
